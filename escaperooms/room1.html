Expedition: Catastrophe


An explorer's plane has crashed recently. You and your friends, being curious go to investigate the site. The scrap parts of the wing fall down behind you, trapping you in the debris. You discover an oil leak and estimate you have about an hour to escape. With the exits blocked and the clock ticking, you must dig through the explorer's journals and maps to figure out where the secret emergency exit is.


Time: 60 Minutes


Difficulty: Easy
